About

I'm a writer from Jamaica who tells character‑driven romance and sci‑fi stories—often with Black women at the heart of the adventure.

I’ve written more than a dozen books across contemporary and speculative romance, including the Modified Mates series. I love Trekkie‑style what‑ifs, swoony connections, and endings that feel earned.

These days I call the UK home, juggling family life with late‑night writing sessions.
